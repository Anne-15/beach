Beach Resort

Beach Resort is an executive resort situated at the coastal area "Bahamas". The area has good weather free of tides and some of the resorts are in islands located near the main shore. The resorts are divided into classes according to their capacity;

  • three-bedroom
  • two-bedroom
  • single room
  • bed-sitter
The Resort offers services to its customers.The room service ensures that you stay in a clean room everyday. The restaurant is there to provide meals at your service. The gym is there to keep you fit. The swimming pool plus the beach is at your service to keep you recreated and entertained and Every Friday we have guest Artists who perform for our guests in the hall. So far we have invited Artists like;
  1. Avicii
  2. Calvin Harris
In addition to that, we offer massage and spa services to our customers any time at the resort. A time to cleanse and feel brand new is here at the Beach Resort.
